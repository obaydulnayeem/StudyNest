What is Onebyzero Edu? Our Objectives Our Resources Types Our Core Features

Onebyzero Edu is an innovative Ed-Tech platform designed specifically for university students in Bangladesh, aiming to serve as a digital and analytical study partner. Our mission is to make education more organized, insightful, and hassle-free by leveraging technology and data analytics.

  • Increasing Peer Collaboration & Connectivity (Foster an environment for easy connection and collaboration among students and educators.)
  • Organizing Educational Resources (Centralize all educational materials to streamline access and reduce redundancy.)
  • Reducing Hassles (Minimize redundant tasks to save time and effort for users.)
  • Providing Institutional Insights, Comparisons & Summary (Offer detailed analytics and comparisons across institutions.)
  • Creating Universal Student Portfolio (Create a comprehensive portfolio for students to track and showcase their academic journey.)

What is Onebyzero Coin?

Onebyzero Coin is a digital reward currency designed to incentivize your engagement and contributions on our platform.

Why Onebyzero Coin?

Our coin system encourages you to actively participate by rewarding you with coins that can be used for various benefits, including accessing premium features of Onebyzero Edu, converting coins to BDT (Bangladeshi Taka), and receiving exclusive gifts.

How does it work?

You can earn Onebyzero Coins by completing specific tasks, such as contributing valuable contents (Questions / Books / Lecture Slides / Notes) or referring new users to the platform. These coins can be redeemed for rewards, converted to BDT, or used to unlock premium features and exclusive gifts within Onebyzero Edu.
